Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.
How can I submit an assignment?
Navigate to your course, find the assignment, and click on "Submit Assignment." Follow the prompts to upload your work.
Where can I view my grades?
You can find your grades in the "Gradebook" section of your dashboard or within each individual course.
